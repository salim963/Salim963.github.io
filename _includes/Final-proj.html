Fire battalions in San Francisco

Battalions are geographic regions within the San Francisco Fire Department's coverage area. Each Battalion have many fire stations is responsible for responding to emergency calls within its assigned area, and is made up of multiple fire stations and firefighting personnel. There are 10 Battalions in San Francisco, and they are identified by a unique code starting with the letter 'B', followed by a two-digit number.

The plot shows the total number of calls for all 10 Battalions in San Francisco by year. From the chart, we can see that the number of calls has been increasing gradually over the years, with the year 2022 having the highest number of calls.

To get a closer look at the data, we can focus on the year 2022, which had the most calls. The bokeh plot can provide insights into which call situations are most common in different neighborhoods, allowing the fire department to allocate their resources and plan their emergency response strategies more effectively.
